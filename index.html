Did You Know?

Visible Light Transmission (VLT) measures the light that passes through glass, so the lower the VLT percentage, the darker the film. Current Victorian regulations (commonly referred to as Darkest Legal) allow for 20% VLT for windows located behind the driver and 35% for driver and front passenger side windows.
